Install steps:
  1. Click Download all 4 PNGs — your browser saves icon16.png, icon32.png, icon48.png, icon128.png to your Downloads folder.
  2. Move all four files into C:\src\BiasBuster\icons\.
  3. Open chrome://extensions and click Reload on the Bias Buster card.
